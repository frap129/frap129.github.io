About Me

Hey, I'm Joe.

I'm a guy who enjoys quality and minimalism in everything I do. Currently, I'm a student
studying under the International Baccalaureate Programme, with a lot of extra-curriculars.


Aspiring Developer

I'm a self-taught code monkey. I work on projects involving Android, Linux, and various compilers, and have been using *NIX systems for upwards of 4 years now. Right now, I prefer systems without a GUI for the sake of performance and practicing bash.


Audiophile

Music has always been a passion of mine, whether I'm playing, listening, or singing along. As a high school student, I have a very low budget, so building and modifying audio hardware has become a hobby of mine.


OpenSource Advocate

I am a strong supporter of personal freedoms, both online and off. OpenSource Projects allow for others to improve your code or sample your changes in their own. Personally, I believe all software should be OpenSource, to an extent.

Large Projects & Contributions

Here is a selection of projects that I've started or contributed to inmy time
working with code. All of these can be found on Github, and the cards below link you to them.

  • DragonTC

    DragonTC is the first OpenSource custom LLVM/Clang toolchain designed with AOSP in mind.

  • SaberMod

    The SaberMod Project is both a custom GCC toolchain and an optimization suite for Android ROMs.

  • RadonX

    RadonX is an Android ROM based off of CAF with further improvments, such as DragonTC and SaberMod.

  • SaberFox

    SaberFox is an Android app based off of Firefox with a darker theme and SaberMod optimizations.

  • FRC 4810

    FRC Team IAMRobot 4810 is the robotics team that I have been a part of for the past 3 years.
