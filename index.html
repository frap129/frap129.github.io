About Me

Hi, I'm Joe.

I'm a guy who enjoys quality and minimalism in everything I do. Currently, I'm a student
studying under the International Baccalaureate Programme, with a lot of extra-curriculars.


Aspiring Developer

I'm a self-taught code monkey. I work on projects involving Android, Linux, and various compilers, and have been using Unix-like/Posix based systems for upwards of 4 years now. Right now, I prefer systems without a GUI for the sake of performance and practicing bash.


Audiophile

Music has always been a passion of mine, whether I'm playing, listening, or singing along. As a high school student, I have a very low budget, so building and modifying audio hardware has become a hobby of mine.


OpenSource Advocate

I am a strong supporter of personal freedoms, both online and off. OpenSource Projects allow for others to improve your code or sample your changes in their own. Personally, I believe all software should be OpenSource, to an extent.

Large Projects & Contributions

Here is a selection of projects that I've started or contributed to inmy time
working with code. All of these can be found on Github, and the cards below link you to them.

  • Atomize

    Atomize is an Android GUI front-end for the popular pngquant PNG compression tool.

  • Spectrum

    Spectrum is a simple, profile based kernel manager for Android devices.

  • CarbonROM

    CarbonROM is a popular feature packed, bloat-free Android ROM.

  • Electron Kernel

    Electron is a kernel for the Nexus 6P featuring my own Chill governor and Maple I/O Scheduler.

  • Pill Widget Colors

    Pill Widget Colors is a clone of the Pixel Pill Widget, with tons of themeing options!

  • FRC 4810

    FRC Team IAMRobot 4810 is the robotics team that I have been a part of for the past 4 years.
